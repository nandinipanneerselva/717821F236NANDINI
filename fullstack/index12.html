Indian street foods are mouth-watering and tempting,but they are often quoted as "unhealthy". But,what if we say they are healthy too?Surprised?Take a look at healthy Indian street foods that are perfect for guilt-free indulgence.

Every region, state, and even city has its own delicacies that are adored by locals that will tell you that the street food in their town is better than anything else you might try in India. And they are all right.
